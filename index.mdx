## What is WiCAN?

WiCAN is an ESP32-based CAN bus adapter available in OBD-II and USB form factors. It bridges your vehicle's CAN bus to WiFi and Bluetooth, enabling real-time diagnostics, data logging, and smart home integration without proprietary software.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to the WiCAN access point">
    Power on your WiCAN by plugging it into the OBD-II port. Connect to the WiFi network named **WiCAN_xxxxxxx** using the default password `@meatpi#`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the configuration interface">
    Navigate to `http://192.168.80.1` in your browser (or `http://192.168.0.10` for WiCAN PRO). You will see the device dashboard with Status, Settings, Automate, and System tabs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to your home network">
    Go to the **Settings** tab, set **Mode** to `AP+Station`, enter your WiFi SSID and password, and click **Submit Changes**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your protocol and start reading data">
    Set the **Protocol** to `AutoPID`, configure your vehicle model on the **Automate** tab, and start receiving live vehicle data via MQTT or HTTP.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Always keep your firmware up to date. Visit the [Firmware Update](/firmware-update) guide to learn how to update via OTA or USB.
</Note>
